How We Work

Two Tracks. One Principle.

Every engagement fits one of two structures, depending on whether your legal need is ongoing or discrete. Both are priced the same way: on the outcome, not the clock.

Fractional General Counsel

An Ongoing Relationship

You’re embedded. Counsel who knows your business, your contracts, and your risk profile before a problem shows up, not after. Pricing is dynamic and value-based, scored through AireGeneral™ against the complexity and stakes of what your business actually needs month to month.

Matter-Based Engagement

A Discrete Engagement

One deal, one dispute, one contract that needs to get done right. Scoped up front, priced against the value at stake using AirePocket™, and closed out when the matter is closed. No retainer required.

What makes both different: we get paid for outcomes, not hours.

Common Questions

What You’re Probably Wondering

How does value-based pricing work?

Instead of billing by the hour, we price against the value and risk at stake in your matter — using tools like AireGeneral™ and AirePocket™ to score complexity and stakes up front. You know the cost before the work starts, not after.

What’s in it for me?

Predictability, alignment, and speed. You’re not watching a clock or worrying that asking a question costs you money. And because we’re only paid for outcomes, the incentive is to solve your problem well, not to generate hours.

How is this different from hourly billing?

Hourly billing rewards time spent, not problems solved. It can quietly discourage you from calling with a quick question. Value-based pricing removes that friction entirely — the price is set by the stakes, not the clock.

Is this still a law firm — do I still have attorney-client privilege?

Yes. Every engagement is a genuine attorney-client relationship, with full privilege protection, delivered by counsel licensed in Georgia and Alabama. Legal counsel, built for business owners who’d rather not need a lawyer.
